Caution: You have descended into Level 1 of the Backrooms, a desolate realm of deteriorating existence. The once familiar surroundings are warped and twisted, revealing the true nature of this eerie domain.

Level 1 is a labyrinthine nightmare of decaying hallways and forgotten chambers. The air is heavy with an oppressive sense of dread, and the sound of distant footsteps and whispers echo through the corroded walls.

Level 1 at the time of initial discovery

As you navigate through this level, the rooms become increasingly surreal and distorted. Reality shifts and bends, playing tricks on your mind and distorting your senses. Shadows dance and writhe, concealing sinister secrets within their depths.

A corridor at Level 1

Warning: Exercise extreme caution within Level 1. The dangers lurking within its confines are far from mere illusions. Entities of malevolence and maleficence may stalk the corridors, eager to ensnare those who dare venture further.

Entering Level 1:You can enter this level from Level 0. There are many entrances at this level; Opening any ordinary door in many levels is highly likely to enter Level 1. In addition, climbing a tree in Level 11.1, completing the fifth event in Level 92, and completing the King's Trial in Level 94 are reliable ways to return to Level 1.

Accessible Levels:You only need to continue exploring the back room to leave Level 1. Wandering along any extended corridor usually leads to Level 2, finding a hole in the wall to enter Level 19. If there is a graffiti cut on the ceiling, you can go to a party!
